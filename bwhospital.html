Brigham and Women's Hospital Software

I was part of a team that developed a hospital operations web application using React, Next.js, AWS, and Agile methodologies for enhanced functionality.

This project was completed in my D Term in 2024 at WPI through the CS 3733 class.

I worked with a team of 10 individuals to facilitate the creation of a web application handling hospital operation, as part of an 8-Week Class exercise.

We employed technologies such as React, NextJS, AWS, and Auth0 to create a full fledged and feature-rich app.

Some of the notable features included :

  • Appointment Scheduling
  • Patient Management
  • Pathfinding within the Hospital
  • Requesting of Hospital Services (such as medical device requests to certain locations)
  • Management of Administrative Tasks for Hospital Staff and Doctors
  • and other features!

    • Our team was able to successfully deliver a fully functional application, that was well received by professor.
